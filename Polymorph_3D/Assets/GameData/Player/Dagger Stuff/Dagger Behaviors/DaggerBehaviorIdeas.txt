Ideas for certain dagger behaviors
1. Lure
	- Player holds dagger to mouth and blows a hitscan "blowdart"
	- This blowdart does not deal any damage, but creates a sound that lures enemies when it impacts a surface
	- The blowdart can stun enemies if they are hit in the eyes
2. Teleport
	- Player throws knife to location they want to teleport to 
	- Player teleports to location (if valid) or nearest valid location within their fov
